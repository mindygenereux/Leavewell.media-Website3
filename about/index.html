Mindy Genereux is a Certified Death Doula and End-of-Life Coach, trained under Alua Arthur and Claire Bidwell Smith — two of the most respected voices in the death doula space. She is the founder of Leave Well Media and the creator of Story Studio, a private app for capturing family stories and building lasting legacies.

Leave Well Media was born at a bedside. Built in grief, shaped by love, and designed so that no family has to lose the voice of the person they adore. Mindy understands the particular ache of anticipatory grief — the strange suspended time where someone you love is still here, and also leaving.

Through the Live Well Library, Story Studio, and the Legacy Shop, Mindy offers families practical tools, emotional resources, and a community that understands. Her work is rooted in one belief: that the story you leave is the love that stays.

The Story Studio Framework

Every person has a
place in the story.

Story Studio was built around four archetypes. Each person in your family has a unique and valuable role to play.

The Hero

Lead the Story

The person whose story is being told. They invite their people, add stories in words, photos, or video, and build the final legacy project.

The Guide

Be the Steady Companion

The caregiver, the coordinator, the one who holds everything together. The Guide helps gather stories, provides support, and assists with organizing memories.

The Builder

Shape the Legacy

Family members and friends who contribute stories, photos, and reflections that make the final keepsake richer and more complete.

The Witness

Hold Space

Those who read, watch, and honor the story as it unfolds. Their presence and attention matters deeply to the Hero and the family.
