Our Story

Built from grief.
Driven by love.

Leave Well Media was created by Mindy Genereux, a Certified Death Doula trained under world-renowned end-of-life educators Alua Arthur and Claire Bidwell Smith. It was not built in a boardroom or funded by investors. It was built at a bedside, in grief, with a singular conviction: that the end of life deserves better tools.

"I watched my family lose time they could never get back. I built Leave Well Media so yours doesn't have to."

What started as a personal mission became a movement. Today Leave Well Media reaches families navigating terminal illness, anticipatory grief, hospice care, and legacy planning with digital resources that are human, creative, and rooted in compassion.

Mindy Genereux · Certified Death Doula · Trained under Alua Arthur & Claire Bidwell Smith
Our Mission and Vision

Helping one million people
approach death with
less fear.

We believe end of life support should not be clinical or cold. It should be human, creative, and rooted in compassion. These are the four pillars that guide everything we build.

🌿

Presence Over Panic

We create tools that bring people back to the present moment. Anticipatory grief thrives in fear of the future. Our resources are designed to reduce that weight and return people to what matters most — right now.

✦

Stories Over Silence

The stories we leave behind are the love that stays. We build platforms and tools that make capturing, organizing, and preserving life stories accessible to every family.

🤝

Connection Over Isolation

Grief is not meant to be carried alone. We are building community spaces where honesty is welcome, grief is not hidden, and the end of life becomes a shared act of care.

💡

Clarity Over Confusion

When people have space to reflect, speak openly, and share their stories, they feel peace rather than pain. Our tools offer structure without pressure and connection without noise.

Our Community

You belong here.
Exactly as you are.

Leave Well Media is more than a resource hub. It is a community of people who believe that grief deserves space, stories deserve to be told, and no one should have to figure this out alone.

01

Honest Conversations Welcome

We believe the most powerful thing you can do at the end of life is speak truthfully. Hard conversations are not just allowed — they are encouraged.

02

Grief Is Not Hidden Here

Anticipatory grief is real, exhausting, and rarely acknowledged. We name it, we hold space for it, and we build every tool with it in mind.

03

Healing Through Community

Powerful conversations reduce blame, shame, and guilt for both the dying and the people who love them.

04

Digital Tools, Deeply Human

We believe technology and humanity are not opposites. Our tools are designed to feel warm, gentle, and safe — especially when emotions are running high.
