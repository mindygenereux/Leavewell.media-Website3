Stop Drowning in the Diagnosis

If your dad has a terminal diagnosis, this is for you.

Fear has the pen right now. It is writing worst-case timelines — and that is not the whole story.

The window is open right now. This is the added chapter, not the ending.

In 8 weeks you go from cancer stole the plot to you write the story anyway.

✦Name the spiral
✦Find him again

✦You write this chapter with him

If his diagnosis has fear writing the story, this is where you start.

The Clarity Map is a free interactive guide that walks you through the four stages from spiral to presence — and a five-page workbook you fill out right in your browser. Circle the words that feel true. Rewrite fear’s narrative. Write him a note. Track your presence level. Come back to the room while the window is still open.

No printing. No downloading. Just you, showing up for him.
