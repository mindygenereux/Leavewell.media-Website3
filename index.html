Story Studio

A simple way to capture stories
that matter.

Story Studio is a private digital space where families can gather meaningful moments, reflections, and memories while navigating terminal illness and anticipatory grief.

One person leads their story. Loved ones contribute in their own words. Everything is organized into a storyboard and keepsake video you can save, share, and return to forever.

Learn More About Story Studio →

🎙️ AI-Guided Story Prompts

50+ prompts plus an AI generator that creates personalized questions based on your unique life and memories.

📅 Scheduled Future Messages

Record messages for birthdays, graduations, and milestones. They arrive exactly when they are needed most.

🔒 100% Private by Design

No social media. No ads. Only the people you personally invite can see your Story Studio.

🎬 Final Tribute Video

Storyboard plus Canva Integration creates a polished tribute video to share with your inner circle.

About Leave Well Media

Changing how people
live before loss.

Leave Well Media was founded by Mindy Genereux, Certified Death Doula and Anticipatory Grief Coach, after sitting beside a dying parent and recognizing how few resources existed for the space between diagnosis and death.

"The goal is not to prepare for death. It is to live fully, right until the end."

This space was built for the people navigating terminal illness alongside someone they love. For those who want to live well, support well, and leave something meaningful behind.
