How it works: Live Well, Love Well, Leave Well

This is an 8-week process. Each week tackles one transformation. You show up. We do the work. You leave knowing you didn't miss this time.

Week 1

Understanding the Diagnosis

You're spiraling. Every search confirms the worst thought. I teach you the diagnosis is one data point, not the whole chapter. You can hear the word "terminal" without your brain shutting off.

Week 2

Making Memories

You think you're out of time. I teach you the window is open RIGHT NOW. You realize there's still time. You stop acting like he's already gone. You start seeing your dad as the HERO and begin capturing memories with a smile.

Week 3

Remembering Who He Is

All you see is the cancer. You remember the whole character. Every level he played before this one. You tell a story about him that includes him being sick. You know the player and how he will get through this next chapter, his way.

Week 4

Reading Between the Lines

He's smiling while hiding the hard parts. I teach you to see past the performance. To read what he's not saying. You show him you can handle it by reading between the lines to observe the truth without saying it.

Week 5

Letting Him Live His Way

He's buying things he won't use. I give you permission to witness without fixing. To let him buy the muffin, the car, the dream. You're the comfort because you trust his process.

Week 6

Supporting Him Through the Hard Parts

You're watching him lose control of things he's always done himself. I teach you how to support him. This is next level love and support.

Week 7

What Cancer Actually Gave You

Everyone says "Fuck Cancer." You're aware cancer isn't the villain, but an alarm clock. It woke you both up and gave you this window to make this chapter matter.

Week 8

The Relationship Continues

You're terrified it's game over when he dies. You trust he's not gone. You know the story continues because his character lives in you. Having fun while being curious and proving you both won.
