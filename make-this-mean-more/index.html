How it works: Live Well, Love Well, Leave Well

This is an 8-week process. Each week tackles one transformation. You show up. We do the work. You leave knowing you didn't miss this time.

Week 1

Drowning in the diagnosis

You're spiraling. Every search confirms the worst thought. I teach you the diagnosis is one data point. Not the whole chapter. You can hear the word "terminal" without your brain shutting off.

Week 2

The window is open

You think you're out of time. I teach you the window is open RIGHT NOW. You realize there's still time. You start seeing your dad as the HERO.

Week 3

Main character energy

You remember the whole character. Every level he played before this one. You tell a story about him that includes him being sick.

Week 4

Connect the dots

He's smiling while hiding the hard parts. I teach you to see past the performance. To read what he's not saying.

Week 5

FOMO

He's buying things he won't use. I give you permission to witness without fixing. To let him buy the muffin, the car, the dream. You're the comfort because you trust his process.

Week 6

Letting go

You're watching him lose control of things he's always done himself. I teach you how to support him. This is next level love and support.

Week 7

The thief in the night

You hear cancer is the villain. You're aware cancer isn't the villain, but an alarm clock. It woke you both up and gave you this window to make this chapter matter.

Week 8

Continue?

You're terrified it's game over when he dies. You trust he's not gone. You know the story continues because his character lives in you.
