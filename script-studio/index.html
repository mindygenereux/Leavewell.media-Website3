How to read these templates
✦
Teal dashed text = a blank that fills from the sidebar. Type in the field and it updates here live.
▸
Pink label badges = grammar hints. They tell you what kind of word or phrase belongs in that blank. They do not disappear — use them as a guide.
▸
Yellow beat markers = a natural pause. Hold it for one full second before continuing. Do not fill the silence.
▸
Grey italic text = camera or delivery direction. It is for your reference only — do not say it on camera.
