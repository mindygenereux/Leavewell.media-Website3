Talking HeadHigh saveAlgorithm loves this

HOOK — 0–3 sec · straight down the lens · no warm-up
You're not scared of the thing they're scared of losing. beat You're scared of losing the one thing you can't get back.
CAM: Medium close-up. Straight to camera. First sentence. Pause. Second sentence. Don't rush it.
STICK — 3–15 sec · say the thing they've never heard out loud
And that's a really specific thing to carry around. beat It's not about <the big scary word for it — e.g. "death," "the diagnosis," "the divorce," "losing the business">. It's about the one thing you can't get back. It's about what disappears when <he/she/they/it> does.
CAM: Stay still. Slow your pace right down — give them time to feel it.
SHIFT — 15–40 sec · the reframe · this is the gift
Here's what I know. beat your one-line truth. beat The people who feel this the hardest are the ones paying the closest attention. That's you. You already know what matters — you've known for a while. You just needed someone to say it.
CAM: Lean in slightly here. Come back to neutral for the last line.
SERVE — 40–55 sec · give them something to actually do
one small thing they can do today. beat It doesn't have to be perfect. It just has to happen.
CAM: Warmer energy here. This is the bit they save and come back to.
CTA — last 5–8 sec · one ask only
your offer was made for this exact moment/chapter/season. your closing line. Link's in my bio.
CAM: Don't pivot into a different energy. Same tone. Just the next sentence.

Product Promo — Bridge Method
60–90 sec · TikTok / Reels · Lead with the problem. Don't mention your offer until 30 seconds in.
Product PromoConverts
▾
WHERE THEY ARE — 0–8 sec · name the stuckness
You know there are things you want to do with this moment/chapter/season. beat Things you want to say, things you want to <verb — e.g. "capture," "create," "record," "share," "get out of your head">. beat But every time you try to start — the thing keeping them stuck.
CAM: Medium close. No urgency. You're sitting next to them — not selling from a stage.
WHERE THEY WANT TO BE — 8–25 sec
What you actually want is what they actually want. beat Something real. Something that doesn't disappear when the <moment/conversation/visit/session/call> ends.
THE BRIDGE — 25–50 sec · introduce what you offer · lead with what it gives them, not what it is
That's what your offer is for. beat It gives who you talk to a way in — without having to figure out where to start on their own. beat You don't have to have it all worked out. That's the whole point.
CAM: Can cut to a screen recording or product walkthrough here if you have one.
PROOF — 50–70 sec · something true · not a pitch
a real result or quote. beat You don't need to wait until you feel ready. You start — and ready shows up after.
CTA — last 8 sec · one ask
Link in my bio. your offer. beat your closing line.
Template 7
Poll Video — Let Them Choose
30 sec · TikTok · Run this BEFORE you build the product. Check comments in 24 hours. Build the winner only.
Poll VideoHigh comment rate
▾
HOOK — 0–4 sec · let them in on something
I'm building something and I need your help picking which one comes first.
CAM: Light, direct, slightly conspiratorial — like you're letting them in on a decision, not asking for a favour.
SETUP — 4–15 sec
Both of these are for who you talk to. Both are your price. I can only make one first — so I'm letting you decide.
THE TWO OPTIONS — 15–25 sec · no pressure either way
Option A: poll option A. beat Option B: poll option B.
CTA — last 5 sec · dead simple
Comment A or B. beat Most votes wins. I'll build it this week.
Don't offer both. One product. The choice between two options is what drives the comment. Check in 24 hours and build whatever won.
