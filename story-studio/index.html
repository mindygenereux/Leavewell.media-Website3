The Archetype Framework

Everyone has a role.
Every role has a purpose.

Story Studio's unique invitation system keeps the HERO in total control — deciding who enters their story and how much they share. Emotional safety isn't an afterthought. It's the architecture.

✦The Center of the Story

The HERO

The person whose legacy is being honored. They lead, they invite, they decide how their story is told — right until the final project is complete.

🧭Closest Companion

The GUIDE

The caregiver or closest support person. They help the HERO tell their story and can complete the legacy when the HERO needs them most.

🛠️Trusted Inner Circle

The BUILDERS

Children, partners, chosen family — they contribute memories and help with the technical side at the HERO's personal invitation.

👁️Extended Community

The WITNESSES

Friends and extended family who contribute at their own pace, honored and included while the HERO stays safely at the center.

Questions Answered

You have questions.
We have you covered.

Is Story Studio only for people who are actively dying?+
Absolutely not. Story Studio is for anyone who wants to preserve a legacy — whether you're facing a terminal diagnosis, supporting someone in hospice, or simply want your stories captured for future generations.
Is my story completely private?+
Yes — 100%. Story Studio is completely disconnected from social media. Nothing is ever shared unless you explicitly choose to share it. Only the people you personally invite can access or contribute to it.
What if I feel overwhelmed or don't know what to say?+
That's exactly what Story Studio was built for. The AI-guided prompts will spark memories you didn't even know you wanted to share. You can start with a 5-minute voice note or a favorite photo. There's no pressure, no timeline, and no wrong way to tell your story.
Can multiple family members contribute?+
Yes! The Archetype Framework was designed exactly for this. The HERO invites Builders and Witnesses who can contribute memories, photos, and stories from their own perspective while the HERO stays in full control.
Do I need to download an app?+
Story Studio works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No complicated downloads required. Visit storystudio.media to begin.
