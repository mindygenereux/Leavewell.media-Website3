What Story Studio Does

Everything you need to
build a living legacy.

Write, Record, or Speak

Capture stories in words, photos, video, or voice. Whatever feels most natural. There is no wrong way.

📅

Scheduled Messages

Write heartfelt messages now and schedule them to arrive on birthdays, graduations, and milestones — even when you cannot be there.

👥

Invite Your People

Bring in family and friends as Hero, Guide, Builder, or Witness. Everyone has a role. Everyone belongs.

Legacy Projects

Build memory books, tribute videos, and keepsakes that will outlive all of you. Created with love, preserved forever.

Completely Private

Your Role in the Story

Every person has a
place here.

Each person in your Story Studio has a unique and valuable role. Together you create something none of you could build alone.

Hero

Lead the Story

Invite your people. Add stories in words, photos, or video, and build the final project.

Guide

Be the Steady Companion

Help gather stories, provide support, and assist with organizing memories.

Builder

Shape the Legacy

Contribute stories, photos, and reflections that make the final keepsake richer.

Witness

Hold Space

Read, watch, and honor the story as it unfolds. Your presence matters deeply.
